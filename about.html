What is SafeChat?

SafeChat ek aisi platform hai jahan aap duniya bhar ke anjaan logon (strangers) se bina kisi registration ya login ke baatein kar sakte hain. Humne ise isliye banaya hai taaki log khul kar apne dil ki baat kar sakein, naye dost bana sakein, aur mukhtalif cultures ke baare mein jaan sakein—wo bhi apni privacy ko khatre mein daale bina.

100% Anonymous

Aapka asli naam, IP address ya email kabhi bhi kisi ko nahi dikhaya jata.

No Logs

Hum chat history save nahi karte. Browser band karte hi messages delete ho jate hain.

Why SafeChat?

Duniya mein hazaron chat sites hain, lekin SafeChat par hamara focus 3 cheezon par hai:

  • Simplicity: Bas enter kijiye aur chatting shuru kijiye.
  • Safety Filters: Hamara AI system personal info share hone se rokta hai.
  • Free Forever: Ye site hamesha free rahegi bina kisi hidden charge ke.
