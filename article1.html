Data Center Cooling: PAC Unit aur Unka Maintenance

Ek Data Center ke liye sabse zaroori cheez hoti hai uska cooling system. Server rooms mein lagatar chalne wale machines bohot zyada garmi (heat) paida karte hain. Agar is garmi ko control nahi kiya gaya, toh servers crash ho sakte hain aur poora network down ho sakta hai. Yahin kaam aata hai Precision Air Conditioning (PAC) unit.

PAC Unit Normal AC se alag kyun hota hai?

Hum gharon mein jo normal AC use karte hain, wo sirf temperature control karte hain. Lekin PAC units temperature ke saath-saath Humidity (nami) aur air filtration ko bhi control karte hain. Ek data center mein 24/7 bina ruke cooling ki zaroorat hoti hai, isliye PAC units ko bohot heavy-duty banaya jata hai.

Pro Tip: PAC units mein set-points ka sahi hona bohot zaroori hai. Aam taur par Data Centers mein temperature 22°C se 24°C ke beech rakha jata hai.

PAC Unit ke Common Alarms aur Troubleshooting

Ek technician ke taur par, aapko in common alarms ka pata hona chahiye:

  • High Temperature Alarm: Iska matlab hai ki room ka temperature set-limit se upar chala gaya hai. Iska karan compressor fail hona ya gas leak ho sakta hai.
  • Air Flow Failure: Agar fan belt toot jaye ya filter block ho jaye, toh air flow ruk jata hai.
  • Water Leakage: PAC unit ke niche pani jama hona dangerous ho sakta hai, isliye drain pipe ki safai zaroori hai.

Maintenance kyun zaroori hai?

Time par maintenance karne se PAC unit ki life badhti hai aur load balanced rehta hai. Filters ki regular safai aur condenser units ki checking se bijli ki khapat (HT readings) bhi kam hoti hai aur system efficient bana rehta hai.

Agar aap ek Data Center technician hain, toh hamesha dhyan rakhein ki load distribution sahi ho taaki kisi ek unit par zyada pressure na pade.
