Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 05, 2026

Safe Chat ("hum", "humein", ya "hamara") aapki privacy ka pura dhyan rakhta hai. Ye page aapko batata hai ki jab aap hamari website waheed-webdev.github.io use karte hain, toh hum kaunsi jaankari collect karte hain.

1. Information Collection

Hamari site ek anonymous chat application hai. Hum aapka naam, phone number, ya koi bhi personal detail store nahi karte hain jab tak aap khud use chat mein na likhein.

2. Log Data

Jaise baaki websites karti hain, hum bhi "Log Data" collect karte hain jaise aapka IP address aur browser version. Ye sirf site ki performance sudharne ke liye hota hai.

3. Cookies aur Google AdSense

Hum apni site par ads dikhane ke liye Google AdSense ka use karte hain. Google "cookies" ka use karta hai taaki wo aapko aapki pasand ke mutabiq ads dikha sake. Aap chahein toh apne browser ki settings mein ja kar cookies ko disable kar sakte hain.

4. Data Security

Aapke messages Firebase database mein save hote hain. Hum poori koshish karte hain ki ye safe rahein, lekin internet par koi bhi cheez 100% secure nahi hoti.

5. Contact Us

Agar aapka koi sawal hai, toh aap humse hamari website ke contact section ke zariye baat kar sakte hain.
