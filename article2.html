SIP Investment: Choti bachat se bada fund kaise banayein?

Bahut se log sochte hain ki share market ya mutual funds mein invest karne ke liye lakhs rupaye chahiye, lekin ye sach nahi hai. Aap sirf ₹500 mahina se bhi apni investment journey shuru kar sakte hain. Iska sabse aasaan tarika hai Systematic Investment Plan (SIP).

SIP kya hai aur ye kaise kaam karta hai?

SIP ka matlab hai ki aap har mahine ek fixed amount mutual fund mein dalte hain. Iska sabse bada fayda hai Rupee Cost Averaging. Jab market niche hota hai, toh aapko zyada units milti hain, aur jab upar hota hai toh kam. Long term mein ye aapke investment ko balance kar deta hai.

Example: Agar aap ₹1,000 har mahine 15% return wale fund mein 20 saal tak dalte hain, toh aapka paisa ₹15 lakh se upar ho sakta hai!

SIP shuru karne ke 3 bade fayde

  • Anushasan (Discipline): Aapke bank account se har mahine apne aap paisa kat jata hai, toh aapko alag se yaad rakhne ki zaroorat nahi padti.
  • Compounding ki taqat: Jitna jaldi aap shuru karenge, utna zyada compounding ka fayda milega.
  • Kam Risk: Ek saath bada paisa lagane ke bajaye thoda-thoda lagane se market ke utaar-chadaav ka darr kam rehta hai.

Investment shuru karne se pehle kya karein?

SIP shuru karne ke liye aapko ek Demat account ya kisi achhe Mutual Fund app ki zaroorat hogi. Hamesha dhyan rakhein ki investment se pehle apna target set karein (jaise bacchon ki padhai ya retirement).

Doston, investment sirf ameer logon ke liye nahi hai, balki ameer banne ke liye hai. Aaj hi apni pehli SIP shuru karein!
