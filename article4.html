Data Center Power: UPS aur Battery Bank ki Dekhbhaal

Data Center mein cooling ke saath-saath Uninterrupted Power Supply (UPS) ka hona sabse zaroori hai. Servers ko ek second ke liye bhi bina power ke nahi chhoda ja sakta. Isliye UPS aur uske saath jude Battery Banks ka sahi salamat rehna technician ki priority hoti hai.

1. Battery Terminal ki Safai

Batteries mein corrosion (shora) jama hona ek aam baat hai. Agar terminals par corrosion jama ho jaye, toh connection loose ho sakta hai aur backup ke waqt system fail ho sakta hai. Inhe regular interval par petroleum jelly lagakar saaf rakhna chahiye.

2. Voltage aur Impedance Testing

Har ek battery ka voltage regular check karna chahiye. Agar kisi ek battery ka voltage kam hai, toh wo poore string ko kharab kar sakti hai. Modern data centers mein Battery Monitoring System (BMS) ka use hota hai jo real-time data deta hai.

Important Tip: UPS room ka temperature hamesha 25°C ke aas-paas rakhein. Zyada garmi se batteries ki life 50% tak kam ho sakti hai.

3. UPS Load Balancing

Hamesha check karein ki UPS par load balanced ho. Agar ek phase par zyada load hoga, toh UPS overheating ki wajah se trip ho sakta hai. HT readings aur load management hi ek achhe technician ki pehchan hai.

Ek technician ke taur par, regular preventive maintenance (PM) hi downtime se bachne ka sabse bada hathiyar hai.
